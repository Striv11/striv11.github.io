Gall stones classification

Ultrasound has been usually used to diagnose the gallbladder stone. For classification data is collected from different laboratories. The data is alienated into input and target data. Target data has two values 1 and 2. 1 will show the effected patients and 2 will show the healthy patients. The purpose being simple, identify gall stones in ultrasound images. Why? Well someone in the family had it, I thought lets give it a small try and see if we can do it with reasonable accuracy!!
